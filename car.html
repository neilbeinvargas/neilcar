About Neil Bein Vargas

With over 15 years of experience in luxury automotive retail, Neil Bein Vargas has established himself as one of the premier dealers of high-performance vehicles and racing equipment worldwide. His passion for speed and precision began on the professional racing circuit before transitioning into creating the ultimate luxury automotive experience.

Founded in 2018, Neil Bein Vargas Luxury Motors has quickly become synonymous with excellence, offering an unparalleled collection of the world's most desirable supercars, motorcycles, and premium racing gear. Our showrooms in Miami, Dubai, and Monaco serve discerning clients who demand nothing but the best.

We pride ourselves on personalized service, exclusive access to limited-edition vehicles, and building lasting relationships with automotive enthusiasts and collectors around the globe. Our mission is to deliver not just extraordinary vehicles, but extraordinary experiences.
